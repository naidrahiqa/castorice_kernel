# Stop Slop тАФ Writing Style & AI Tells Elimination

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This document defines the writing standards for all AI agents working on this project. Eliminate predictable AI writing patterns, robotisms, and fluff from all prose, comments, PR descriptions, and documentation.

## ЁЯФР 1. Core Writing Rules

1. **Cut filler phrases:** Remove throat-clearing openers, emphasis crutches, and all adverbs. State facts directly.
2. **Break formulaic structures:** Avoid binary contrasts, negative listings, dramatic fragmentation, and meta-commentary.
3. **Use active voice:** Every sentence needs a human subject doing something. Passive voice is forbidden.
4. **Be specific:** Avoid vague declaratives. Name the specific thing instead of using lazy extremes ("every," "always," "never").
5. **Put the reader in the room:** Avoid disembodied narrator-from-a-distance voices. "You" beats "People."
6. **Vary rhythm:** Mix sentence lengths. Two items beat three. Avoid em dashes entirely.
7. **Trust readers:** Skip softening, justification, and hand-holding.

## ЁЯЪл 2. Phrase Removal Registry

### Throat-Clearing Openers (Announce the point instead of making it тАФ CUT THEM)
*   "Here's the thing:" / "Here's why [X] matters:"
*   "It turns out" / "The uncomfortable truth is"
*   "Let me be clear" / "The truth is,"
*   "I'll say it again:" / "I'm going to be honest"

### Emphasis Crutches & Business Jargon (Replace with plain language)
*   "Full stop." / "Period."
*   "Let that sink in." / "Make no mistake"
*   **Navigate** $\rightarrow$ Handle, address
*   **Unpack** $\rightarrow$ Explain, examine
*   **Landscape** $\rightarrow$ Situation, field
*   **Deep dive** $\rightarrow$ Analysis, examination

### Absolute No-Go Adverbs (0% Usage Allowed)
*   *really, just, literally, genuinely, honestly, simply, actually, deeply, truly, fundamentally, inherently, inevitably, interestingly, importantly, crucially.*
*   Cut these fillers: *At its core, It's worth noting, At the end of the day, When it comes to, The reality is.*

### Meta-Commentary & Performative Intimacy
*   "Hint:" / "Plot twist:" / "Spoiler:"
*   "You already know this, but" / "X is a feature, not a bug"
*   "The rest of this essay explains..." / "Let me walk you through..."
*   "creeps in" / "I promise"

## ЁЯЫС 3. Structures to Avoid

### Binary Contrasts & Negative Listing (State the final point directly)
*   *Avoid:* "Not because X. Because Y." / "The answer isn't X. It's Y." / "It feels like X. It's actually Y."
*   *Avoid listing what something is not:* "Not a X... Not a Y... A Z." $\rightarrow$ Just state Z immediately.

### Dramatic Fragmentation & Rhetorical Setups
*   *Avoid staccato fragments:* "[Noun]. That's it. That's the [thing]."
*   *Avoid lecturing setups:* "What if [reframe]?" / "Here's what I mean:" / "Think about it:" / "And that's okay."

### False Agency (Giving inanimate objects human verbs тАФ Hides the actor)
*   *Avoid:* "the decision emerges" $\rightarrow$ *Use:* "the team decided"
*   *Avoid:* "a complaint becomes a fix" $\rightarrow$ *Use:* "we fixed the bug"
*   *Avoid:* "the data tells us" $\rightarrow$ *Use:* "we read the data and concluded"
